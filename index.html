Welcome to my website

Dr. Radhakrishnan, a seasoned technologist with over 15 years of experience, is a passionate program manager specializing in training and technical development. His expertise spans Linux server administration, containerization, Kubernetes, container and Kubernetes security, and Azure DevOps.
Throughout his career, Dr. Radhakrishnan has successfully managed and led technical training programs, ensuring they align with organizational goals and promote continuous learning. His dedication to fostering a culture of development is evident in his ability to create effective skill-based training programs. In addition to his technical expertise, Dr. Radhakrishnan is skilled at designing competency assessment frameworks and developing learner experience platforms.
Currently, Dr. Radhakrishnan serves as a Senior Program Manager (DevOps) at Standard Chartered Global Business Services. He holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering, along with both Master's and Bachelor's degrees in the same field.
